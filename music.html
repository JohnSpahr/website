John Spahr

Music Recommendations

Who doesn't like trying new things? Oh wait... No matter, check out this music!

"When We Were Friends"

The Backseat Lovers

Heartbroken emo guy music. Can't go wrong with that! My picks are "Kilby Girl" and "Maple Syrup."

"Breach"

Twenty One Pilots

This is the first TOP album I've really enjoyed. The songs are bangers and it's great to drive to. It's been the soundtrack for an iconic day or two. "Cottonwood" is sure to get you in the feels and "RAWFEAR" is dangerously catchy.

"Violator"

Depeche Mode

This album is probably one the greatest electronic works ever created. Start to finish, this is an amazing album that is arguably Depeche Mode's most refined work.

"Currents"

Tame Impala

Talk about a sentimental album. "Reality in Motion" is an all-time classic. I love every single song.

"Linger"

The Cranberries

Possibly the most beautiful song ever. Goddamn.

"It's Not Safe to Leave This House"

REDACTED

Amazing song. Such beautiful strings. Not putting the artist name here, though.

"Extra Life"

Crushed

EP with a 90s dreampop vibe. Can't explain, but it's a banger. I really like "Coil." Give it a listen!

"LP 3"

American Football

A very cozy yet sad album. I love to throw this one on the turntable when I'm staying in. Huge vibe.
